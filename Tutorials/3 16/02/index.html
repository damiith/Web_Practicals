The history of Nepal

Culture

A typical Nepalese
meal is Dal bhat. Dal
is a spicy lentil soup,
served over bhat
, served with tarkari
together with achar
or chutni. It consists
of non-vegetarian as
well as vegetarian
items served with
non-alcoholic
beverages.
